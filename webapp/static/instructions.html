How XP and Achievements Work

XP formula

Your XP for each session is calculated as:

base(10) + duration_minutes(1/min) + accuracy_bonus(×20) + streak_bonus(×2, max 20) + new_pose_bonus(25 once per pose)

  • Base: Every session awards 10 XP.
  • Duration: 1 XP per minute practiced.
  • Accuracy: Accuracy score × 20.
  • Streak: 2 XP per current streak day (capped at 20).
  • New pose: +25 XP the first time you practice a new pose.

Levels

Levels increase with total XP. Example milestones:

  • Level 2 – 100 XP
  • Level 3 – 300 XP
  • Level 4 – 600 XP
  • Level 5 – 1000 XP
